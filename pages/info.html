Did you know?

Whenever someone sends you an email, you can use Sherpademo's services to send an SMS message to your mobile number containing whatever the content of the email is? Please refer to the example below.

Note: Settings may vary depending on your mail service provider.

1. Create a filter with your email address plus a suffix "+sms" (without quotation marks).

2. Click add a forwarding address.

3. Enter your Mobile number and add "@cs-development-playground.appspotmail.com" (without quotation marks).

4. After successfully adding your mobile number, you will receive a verfication code. Enter the verification code to successfully use the Sherpademo's SMS service.
